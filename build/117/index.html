Best in the West died on 1/3/16 in Pensacola at Pensacola Greyhound Racing. This race track has the fourth lowest (tied with Palm Beach Kennel Club) number of reported racing-dog deaths since 2013.

Derby Lane in St. Petersburg has highest number of reported deaths – 57 – and Naples-Ft. Myers has the lowest number of reported deaths – just three.
